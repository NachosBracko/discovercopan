About Copán

Copán was a significant Mayan city, known for its art and architecture. The city's stelae and hieroglyphs tell tales of kings and rituals from centuries ago.

Attractions

  • Copán Ruins: Explore the Hieroglyphic Stairway and the Great Plaza.
  • Macaw Mountain Bird Park: A sanctuary for macaws, parrots, and other local birds.

Accommodations

  • Hotel Marina Copán: Offers a blend of comfort and history.
  • Hostal La Casa de Café: Budget-friendly with a cozy vibe.

Activities

  • Guided Tours: Learn from local experts about Mayan culture.
  • Nature Walks: Discover the rich biodiversity around Copán.

Plan Your Visit

Best Time to Visit: Visit between December and May for the best weather.

Getting There: Buses from San Pedro Sula or flights to San Pedro Sula followed by a local transfer.
